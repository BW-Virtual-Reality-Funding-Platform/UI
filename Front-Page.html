Introducing the VRV2

A NEW TYPE OF VIRTUAL REALITY

The VRV2 is the product of the best engineers in the Virtual Reality business. This Headset contains the latest, cutting-edge Screen technology. A newly designed screen that completely eliminates all nausea. With the new sensors inside each pair of VRV2 headsets, your screen detect eye-strain and automatically adjust its colors and brightness, reducing itchy-eyes, headaches, and fatigue!

The Story

Our product derrived from our love for Virtual Reality. We knew that VR was going to be the future of media, and we were excited for what it could do. After VR became more available to the public, we quickly started to realize, that an overwhelming ammount of people could not use VR because of problems like motion sickness, eye-strian, and many others. We believe that VR should be for everyone, and made to be used by everyone. That is why we have worked countless days and nights to create the VRV2. We hope you enjoy it.
